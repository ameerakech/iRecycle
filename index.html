Vision

Our vision is to create a world where recycling is an integral part of everyday life, empowering individuals and communities to make sustainable choices. We aspire to lead the way in transforming waste management practices, fostering a culture of environmental responsibility, and inspiring collective action towards a circular economy.

Mission

ABOUT US

iReycle is dedicated to transforming textile waste into valuable resources contributing to a circular economy and reducing landfill burden.
We offer comprehensive textile recycling services from collection and sorting to processing and supplying recycled materials.
Our platform connects users with local recycling options for over 350 materials, making it easier than ever to recycle responsibly.
By leveraging technology, we aim to educate and engage individuals about the importance of sustainability and proper waste management.
Our commitment to preserving the environment drives us to provide seamless and reliable recycling solutions, ensuring that every user can contribute to a greener planet.
